Match rules

-One island per team

-No static cup

-No heating up

-Redemption is shoot until you miss

-One re rack per game (Does NOT take away your redemption priveleges if you re-rack)

-If your opponents put you on redemption, then you can still re rack if you have not used it yet

-If a ball bounces off a cup and goes into another cup, it is only one cup

-If a ball bounces off of the opposing teams body and goes in, thats 2 cups bitch

-Trick shots are anything other than shooting with your opposite hand

-If you knock over any of your own cups, then they get taken out of the game

-You cannot chuck a fastball at your opponents cups just to knock them over, cups will be reset and shot will be retaken (2nd violation in the same game will result in a cup).

-If you swat your opponents ball away before it bounces then they get a reshot (2nd violation will result in a cup)

-If you call island and make it in a different cup than that cup does NOT get taken away

-If you get the ball back on the table, you must trickshot. If you make the trickshot then thats two cups bitch
